Step 1
Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone.
Step 2
Tap on the automations tab at the bottom of the screen.
Step 3
Tap on the plus sign located on the top right of the screen.
Step 4
Tap "Create Personal Automation".
Step 5
Scroll down and tap "App"
Step 6
Scroll down and tap "App", then select "Is Opened"
Step 7
Tap "choose" and select all apps you would like to track.
Step 8
Tap "choose" and select all apps you would like to track.
Step 9
Tap "Done", "Next" and "Add Action"
Step 10
Search for "Get Contents of URL" and enter this URL: "https://my-api-name-one.vercel.app/api/v1/number?param=A"
Step 11
Using the menu above the keyboard, select "Current Date" and change the date format to ISO861. Tick off ISO 8601 Time.
Step 12
Add a second variable for "Device Details", and select "Device Hostname" in the list below.
Step 13
Tap "done", "edit automation", and untick "Ask Before Running"
Step 14
Tap "Done" and repeat all steps again, except change step 6 to "Is closed" and change the URL in step 10 to "https://my-api-name-one.vercel.app/api/v1/number?param=B"
